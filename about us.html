About Us

At Crayon Prix, we are a vibrant institution based in Harare committed to nurturing young minds and fostering excellence in education.

Our dedicated team of educators works diligently to create a creative and innovative environment that inspires growth.

Our mission is to empower our students with the skills and values needed to succeed, while staying true to the rich cultural traditions of Harare.
