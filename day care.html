Our offer to you

🕒 Full-Day Service

Our daycare operates from 7:00 AM to 4:30 PM with an extended care option available. We focus on:
✅ Loving care and attention
✅ Early development
✅ Safe spaces for exploration

👶 Age Group

We specialize in daycare for infants & toddlers ages 18 months - 3 years, offering:
- Gentle guidance and support
- Social and emotional development
- Sensory play and exploration

🍽️ Healthy Meals – Fueling Growth

Good nutrition is key to a child's development! Our meal plan includes:
✅ Balanced meals
✅ Fresh fruits, vegetables, and whole grains
✅ Hydration and healthy snacks

🚐 Transport – Safe & Reliable

We offer secure transportation to ensure a smooth transition from home to daycare. Our trained drivers follow strict safety protocols for peace of mind.
